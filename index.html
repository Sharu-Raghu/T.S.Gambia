Training Ship Gambia is an Auckland based Sea Cadet Unit. We offer young people aged 13 to 18 an opportunity to be part of a great organisation.

    		•

    Our objective is to foster strong leadership skills and a real sense of citizenship in all of our Sea Cadets. Providing a disciplined environment for Cadets to learn in, we also place a lot of importance on having fun and providing young people with as many opportunities as possible.

    We invite you to explore our website which showcases our Sea Cadets in action participating in a range of different activities.

    Joining us is easy, so please contact us, for more information about becoming a Sea Cadet .

    Training Ship Gambia Sea Cadets would like to say a big Thank You to our Sponsors for helping us to fund vital training equipment for our Sea Cadets. For more information, please visit our Sponsors page.
