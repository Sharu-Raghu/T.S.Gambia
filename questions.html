Information about joining T.S. Gambia Sea Cadets

How old do I have to be to join Sea Cadets?

New cadets must be at least 13 years old and no older than 16, however cadets may stay at the unit until they are 19 years old.

What will I gain by joining New Zealand Cadets Force?

  • Make new friends
  • Be a part of a team
  • Experience Challenges
  • Try new things
  • Learn to take responsibility
  • Have fun

How often do TS Gambia Sea Cadets meet? Starts here -->

TS Achilles has what's called a parade night every Thursday night during the school term from 6.30pm - 9pm. We meet approximately every second weekend for activities such as sailing, camping, regattas, shooting and competitions. The days and frequencies of weekend activities vary - usually due to the time of year.

I am interested in getting involved as an Instructor or Volunteer at TS Gambia. How do I find out more?

We are always on the lookout for people to help out at TS Achilles Sea Cadets.

These voluntary roles include assisting with training of Sea Cadets in various skills, organising and running activities, and supervising sailing, outdoor activities and camps.

All of the Instructors and Officers at TS Achilles are volunteers. If you are interested in finding out more about getting involved in training youth for the future, please contact TS Achilles now to find out more.
